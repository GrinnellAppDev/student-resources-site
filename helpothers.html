GC Student Resources - Assisting Others

Do you need help with assisting students who are...

...disruptive and distressed? ...victims of sexual harassment or misconduct? ...victims of bias-based incidents? ...something else?

If a student:
  • Threatens to injure, harm, kill, or risk the safety of self or others
  • Acts in a frightening or threatening manner
  • Refuses to leave the classroom after being asked to leave
  • Reports or initiates a threat or bomb scare
  • Needs emergency medical attention
If a student:
  • Acts significantly out of character
  • Acts in a way that causes alarm
  • Displays unhealthy or dangerous patterns of behavior
If a student:
  • Appears to be out of touch with reality
  • Reports suicidal thoughts, depression, hopelessness, or anxiety
  • Indicates a death of a close family or friend and is having diffulty dealing with grief
  • Indicates a natural disaster, political upheavel, or crisis in home country
  • Reports having been a victim of stalking
  • Reports relationship abuse or violence
  • Reports sexual assault or other sexual misconduct
  • Indicates experiencing incidents of bias or discrimination
  • Exhibits behavior that interferes with instruction or office procedures
  • Exhibits behavior that is lewd or offensive
  • Reports having been a victim of a crime
  • Is having academic difficulty
  • Requests an accomodation due to a disabling condition
  • Is not attending class for an extended period of time
  • Seems overwhelmed by a problem that could affect College persistence
  • Needs help with time management, study skills, or test preparation
